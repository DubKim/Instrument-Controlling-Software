Before you use this application, you must change some settings from files. 

1. Go to AnalogInputThread.py and change the code for you file into your current directory. 
2. Go to GUI_Design2.py and change the directory of the icon into your current directory. 
3. In the AnalogInputThread, you can edit the variable for sample rates. But you must increase the buffer size. 

If you have troubles understanding this code:
1. Search for live-plotting pyqtgraph from Google to understand the mechanism of live-plotting.
2. Go to youtube and find Deusdies2 for PyQt GUI tutorials.
3. Read libnidaqmx and take a look at them. It will be a great help.
